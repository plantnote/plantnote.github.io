Q. How to sync data on multiple devices?

Plantnote app doesn't support that feature.

I know most recent apps support automatic data sync between multiple devices with cloud database servers and associated users accounts. As a part-time individual developer, it's not easy for me to maintain the database server. I'm really sorry for that but you can transfer data when you change phones with backup & restore features.

On the other hand, an app with a local database has some advantages such as fast data access and no cellular data usage. Users may share data on multiple devices with backup & restore features but I don't recommend that way.
