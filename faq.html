Q. How to sync data on multiple devices?

Sorry but the app doesn't support that feature.

Most recent apps support automatic data sync between multiple devices with cloud database servers and associated users accounts. As a part-time individual developer, it's not easy for me to maintain the database server. However you can transfer data when you change phones with backup & restore features.
